Welcome to Pathfinders!

This page you now find yourself in is to serve as your introduction to [Project Name], a handy web application deisgned to act as an quick and easy character creator and simulator for the Pathfinder tabletop role playing game.

For those unfamiliar with the name, "Pathfinder" is a popular third-party tabletop-based role playing game, made by Paizo Publishing. Like its first-party predecessor: Dungeons and Dragons from Wizards of the Coast, Pathfinder takes place in a setting full of fantastical heroes, magicians, monsters, and gods. Proceeding down a campaign of your choosing, you and your fellow players embark on adventures through this world, overcoming challenges and seizing great riches. The failure or success of your actions is determined by the results of various dice rolls, further modified by the stats and skills of your own party, and the traits of the enemies you face along the way.

In this application, you can design your character in line with the in-game character sheets. You can save these characters to your device, and also use them in this site's battle simulator, which will pit your character against an in-game enemies, such as the Sea Snake, Devil Dog, Dark Slayer, and Aboleth, among others, and relay the results back to you in text form.
